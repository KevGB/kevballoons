Get the Best for Your Party

Young, fun, and modern balloon art

Bored of the same old balloon dogs and swords? Kevin has spent over 10 years expanding his balloon twisting skills, and now he is one of the top balloon artists in the world!
He twisted balloons in Croatia, Mexico, Botswana, California, South Africa, Tennessee, Bulgaria, and even at 30,000 ft... just in 2022 alone! If you want a world-famous balloon artist to be the life of your next event, book him while you can!
